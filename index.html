Steve Jobs


Steve Jobs was a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer era. With Steve Wozniak, Jobs founded Apple Inc. in 1976 and transformed the company into a world leader in telecommunications.

Elon Musk


Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and businessman who founded X.com in 1999 (which later became PayPal), SpaceX in 2002 and Tesla Motors in 2003.

Jeff Bezos


Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos reclaimed his title as the world's richest person after he leapfrogged Tesla CEO Elon Musk, according to real-time data from Forbes.
